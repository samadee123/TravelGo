THE SACRED CITY OF KANDY




Kandy is one of the most beautiful cities in Sri Lanka and lies in the midst of hills. Kandy is the second largest city in the country and is frequently visited by Buddhists.

Kandy is very famous among tourist for three main reasons:
1.It is home to the sacred tooth relic of the Buddha (Dalada Maligawa)
2.The Botanical Gardens (Located in Peradeniya)
3.Esala Perahara



Province : Central Province
District : Kandy District
Distance : 114km from Colombo
Popular : Dalada Maligawa
Weather : 27 °C
Type : Cultural, Historical

PLACES TO VISIT




Dalada Maligawa : The temple of the Sacred tooth Relic, where Gautama Buddha's left Canine tooth is kept, is a world-renowned center of worship. UNESCO designated the temple, which is visited daily by hundreds of local and foreign worshippers and visitors, as a world heritage site in 1988.






Peradeniya Botanical Garden : This is a unique gift of mother nature with its ancient yet beautiful landscape. Over 4000 distinct plant species may be found in the gardens, which are considered among Asia's best occupying 59 hectares (146 acres).
